Why Choose Jananam

In this fast paced world what most of us seek is personalized care and answers to the most difficult questions. From the moment you enter our hospital to the the moment you step out, we strive to give unwavering individualized care and crystal clear transparency. Each member of our team cherishes in the miracle that is birth. This leads to them working day in and day out towards making this miracle happen for couples. To aid this unwavering intent we are equipped with ISAR & ISO 9001 certifications. Our clean room labs are clad with antimicrobial wall cladding and are primed for optimal performance which ensures that only quality embryos are prepared. At the end of the day this results in our great success rates across different types of fertility treatments. Our success rates have always been on the upward trend thanks to our constant efforts to keep pace with newer and advanced technologies
